Edible Cookie Dough

Description

Cookie dough is fucking delicious, but sadly a forbidden fruit. You want to eat it, but people tell you not to because you could "get salmonella." Preposterous!

What are you to do? Make some edible cookie dough is what. Here's a recipe for just that!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Beat with an electric mixer until creamy.
  3. Beat in vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Add flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  5. Mix in milk.
  6. Fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
